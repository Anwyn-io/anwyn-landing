ANWYN

The sovereign walled garden for your digital footprint.

Built on Midnight & World Mobile

The Problem

Data is the new oil—the fuel for the AI economy. We generate it daily simply by existing and interacting. This includes health, financial, and environmental data collected by our mobile devices. Today, this data is worth trillions to big tech companies training AIs that may eventually displace human workers. Current DePIN ecosystems force a "Privacy Tax," requiring users to expose location and hardware metadata to earn rewards, leaving your digital life vulnerable.

The Solution

Anwyn utilizes Midnight’s Zero-Knowledge technology to create a Shadow Vault—a walled garden of private data that is securely encrypted but verifiable via ZK proofs. This empowers "Selective Disclosure," proving network value without revealing identity or location. By running the Anwyn app or node on World Mobile, you secure your private data and get paid to contribute anonymized, high-quality insights to solve real-world problems.
